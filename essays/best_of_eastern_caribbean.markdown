- White House Bay, St. Kitts (38kn+)
- Canouan (35kn+)

#### Worst Anchorges (all relative...refers to anchorages only, not the islands themselves)

- Mustique -- VERY rolly
- Marie Gallante -- just not very nice (according to Markus)
- Wallilabou, St. Vincent -- a bit precarious (according to Nana)
- Roseau, Dominica -- just not very nice, scenery or anchorage
- Simpson Bay, St, Maarten -- rolly, scenery (or lack thereof)
- English Harbour, Antigua -- crowded, rolly, bad holding


#### Places to spend more time in

- Carriacou
- Barbuda

#### Nicest Sailing Days

- Southbound: Antigua to Guadeloupe, Union Island to Carriacou
- Northbound: Dominica to Les Saintes, Les Saintes to Pigeon Island,
overnight to Culebra

#### Nice Surprises

- Carriacou

- Bequia (according to Nana)
